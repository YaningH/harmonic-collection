TALK TO

SOMEONE

As you grow comfortable expressing your inner feelings through journaling, consider sharing them with someone you trust. Whether it’s a close friend, family member, or therapist. Talking it out can break the cycle of overthinking, bringing a fresh perspective to what feels overwhelming. They may remind you of your strength, share their own insights from their past experience, or simply offer a safe space where you can release your emotions without judgment. By opening up, you build a bridge of connection and comfort, filling the emptiness that often follows heartbreak and gently guiding you toward healing.
